RE-IMAGINING IDENTITY

a fresh take on technolgap's visual brand

Starting in June 2024, I have been directing the visual rebranding for Technolgap, an Ottawa-based student tech organization. As the Director of Design and Visual Communications, I spearheaded the redesign of the organization’s logo, developed a new colour palette, and crafted a comprehensive branding guide. My role currently involves refreshing Technolgap’s brand identity to enhance its online presence and ensure consistency across all visual materials.
